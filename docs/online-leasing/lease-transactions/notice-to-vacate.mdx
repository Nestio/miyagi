# Notice to Vacate

The Notice to Vacate module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing streamlines the lease termination process, ensuring proper documentation, compliance with legal requirements, and smooth move-out coordination.

## Overview

Our digital notice to vacate system simplifies the termination process for both property managers and residents, ensuring all legal requirements are met and the transition is handled efficiently.

## Key Features

### Digital Notice Submission
- **Online notice forms** - Submit termination notices digitally
- **Legal compliance** - Ensure proper notice periods and requirements
- **Documentation tracking** - Maintain complete termination records
- **Status updates** - Real-time notification of process status

### Notice Types
- **Standard termination** - End of lease term notices
- **Early termination** - Breaking lease agreements
- **Non-renewal notices** - Declining lease renewal
- **Emergency termination** - Immediate termination scenarios

### Move-out Coordination
- **Move-out scheduling** - Coordinate final inspections
- **Key return process** - Track key and access device returns
- **Final walkthrough** - Digital inspection documentation
- **Utilities coordination** - Transfer or disconnect services

## Notice Process

### 1. Notice Submission
