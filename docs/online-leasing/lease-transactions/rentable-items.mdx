# Rentable Items

The Rentable Items module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides comprehensive management of additional rental options such as parking, storage, pets, and other community-specific amenities.

## Overview

Rentable items are additional services or amenities that can be added to a lease agreement for an additional fee. These items enhance the resident experience while providing additional revenue opportunities for property managers.

## Key Features

### Rentable Items Request Selection
- **Rental Item Selection (optional/custom per community)**
    - Applicants may select rental items; however, the specific assignment of rental options (such as parking and storage) is handled by the agent through the "Manage Application" page.
        - Funnel syncs with Yardi to verify the availability of inventory for items such as parking spaces and storage units (Requires Yardi Rentable Item configuration - Funnel's preferred method)
    - Currently Included Inventory Options:
        - Parking: Multiple parking types like garages, carports, and assigned or unassigned parking may be available, depending on the community.
        - Storage: Various storage options like small, medium, or large storage rooms may be available, depending on the community.
        - Wine cooler

### Inventory Management
- **Real-time inventory checking** for available rentable items
- **Automatic availability updates** based on PMS integration
- **Inventory reservation** during application process
- **Release of inventory** for cancelled or denied applications

### Fee Management
- **Automatic fee calculation** for selected rentable items
- **Configurable fee structures** based on community policies
- **Fee adjustments** during lease modifications
- **Payment integration** for rentable item fees

## Pet Management

### Specific to Pets
- **When an applicant submits their application. In the application, the following fields are collected:**
    - Breed (Dogs)
    - Weight
    - Name (refer to the section below for further details)
    - Service Animal
- **Warning messages can be generated by the system** if a pet's weight exceeds the community's specified restrictions.
- **Funnel can house pet policy information within the system** and it can be displayed to applicants while they are entering their pet details.

### Pet Policy Integration
- **Community-specific pet policies** are displayed during application
- **Breed restrictions** and weight limits are enforced
- **Service animal documentation** requirements
- **Pet fee calculations** based on policy

### Pet Documentation
- **Pet registration** and documentation requirements
- **Vaccination records** and health certificates
- **Service animal verification** and documentation
- **Pet agreement** and addendum generation

## Rentable Items Workflow

### 1. Item Selection
- **Applicants select desired rentable items** during application process
- **Real-time availability checking** for selected items
- **Fee calculation** and display to applicant
- **Temporary reservation** of selected items

### 2. Agent Review and Assignment
- **Agents review rentable item selections** through Manage Application page
- **Final assignment** of rentable items based on availability
- **Fee adjustments** and confirmations
- **Inventory updates** in PMS

### 3. Lease Integration
- **Rentable items are included** in lease generation
- **Fee structures** are incorporated into lease terms
- **Addenda generation** for specific rentable items
- **Payment scheduling** for rentable item fees

### 4. Ongoing Management
- **Rentable item modifications** during lease term
- **Fee adjustments** for changes in rentable items
- **Inventory tracking** and management
- **Renewal considerations** for rentable items
