# Renewal Workflow

The Renewal workflow in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides a comprehensive solution for managing lease renewals, from offer generation to final execution, ensuring a seamless experience for both property managers and residents.

## Overview

Our digital renewal system streamlines the entire renewal process, from initial offer generation to final lease execution, providing flexibility and automation while maintaining compliance with all applicable regulations.

## Key Features

### Ingestion of Yardi-Approved Lease Offers
- **The system allows for the seamless ingestion of Yardi-approved lease offers**, streamlining the process. The typical sync times are at 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM, and 8:00 PM EST.
- **Real-time synchronization** with property management systems
- **Automatic offer validation** and processing
- **Error handling** for failed synchronization attempts

### Lease Detail Management
- **Users can add, remove, and manage lease details**, including roommates, rentable items, and fees.
- **Comprehensive household management** during renewal process
- **Flexible rentable item modifications** based on availability
- **Fee structure adjustments** as needed

### Automated Task Configuration
- **Automated tasks can be configured to trigger upon completion of specific events**, ensuring that data management processes are efficiently transferred to the property management system (PMS).
- **Customizable workflow automation** based on community needs
- **Integration with existing task management systems**
- **Role-based task assignments** and notifications

### Renewal Lease Generation
- **Renewal leases are automatically generated for e-signature at the click of a button**, including all required addenda.
- **Template-based lease generation** ensures consistency
- **Automatic addenda inclusion** based on lease details
- **Digital signature workflow** for all parties

## Renewal Process Workflow

### 1. Offer Generation and Approval
- **Lease offers are generated in Yardi** based on community policies
- **Offers are approved** by authorized personnel
- **Offers are synchronized** to Funnel at scheduled intervals
- **Residents are notified** of available renewal options

### 2. Resident Decision Making
- **Residents receive notification** of renewal offers
- **Multiple communication channels** including email, SMS, and in-app
- **Clear presentation** of renewal terms and options
- **Decision deadline** management and reminders

### 3. Renewal Application Processing
- **Residents complete renewal application** with updated information
- **Screening requirements** are determined based on renewal type
- **Documentation updates** are collected as needed
- **Application review** and approval process

### 4. Lease Generation and Execution
- **Renewal lease is automatically generated** with all required addenda
- **Digital signature workflow** is initiated for all parties
- **Agent countersignature** completes the process
- **System updates** are synchronized with PMS

## Automated Marketing Triggers

The renewal workflow integrates with Funnel's automated marketing trigger system to provide timely communications and task management:

- **Renewal Executed** - Triggers when a Renewal is executed via Funnel Online Leasing
- **Renewal Voided** - Triggers when a Renewal is voided via Funnel Online Leasing
- **Remind Resident to make Renewal/NTV/MTM Decision** - Triggers at a specified time relative to Lease End Date if the resident has not selected an offer or made a decision around renewals
- **Renewal Awaiting Document Signatures** - Triggers when a renewal has pending signatures on a document sent
- **Renewal Awaiting Decision** - Triggers when a lease is within its renewal threshold period but no decision has been made. Will re-trigger if a Renewal or Vacate decision is cancelled during the renewal period. Only works if lease offers is NOT enabled
- **Renewal Canceled** - Triggers when a renewal is canceled
- **MTM Offer Needed Before Lease Expires** - Triggers before a lease expires no MTM offer is detected
- **Holdover Offer Needed Before Lease Expires** - Triggers before a lease expires no Holdover offer is detected
