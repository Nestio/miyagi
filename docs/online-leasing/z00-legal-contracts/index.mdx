# Legal and Contracts

The Legal and Contracts module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing ensures legal compliance and provides comprehensive contract management throughout the leasing process.

## Overview

Our legal and contract management system streamlines document creation, digital signing, and legal compliance while ensuring all processes meet state and federal requirements.

## Key Features

### Legal Compliance
- **State and local compliance** - Jurisdiction-specific legal requirements
- **Fair housing compliance** - Non-discriminatory practices
- **Document compliance** - Required lease provisions and disclosures
- **Audit trails** - Complete legal record keeping

### Contract Management
- **Automated document generation** - Template-based document creation
- **Digital signature processing** - Legally binding electronic signatures
- **Version control** - Document version tracking and management
- **Secure storage** - Encrypted document storage and access
