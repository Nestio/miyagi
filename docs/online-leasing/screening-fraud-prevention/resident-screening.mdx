# Resident Screening

The Resident Screening module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides comprehensive background checks, rental history verification, and screening services to help property managers make informed leasing decisions.

## Overview

Our digital screening system streamlines the background check process, providing comprehensive reports while ensuring compliance with fair housing laws and protecting applicant privacy.

## Key Features

### Background Check Services
- **Criminal background checks** - Comprehensive criminal history screening
- **Credit history reports** - Financial responsibility assessment
- **Rental history verification** - Previous landlord references
- **Employment verification** - Income and employment stability

### Screening Types
- **Standard screening** - Basic background and credit checks
- **Enhanced screening** - Comprehensive background investigation
- **Corporate screening** - Business entity verification
- **International screening** - Foreign background checks

### Automated Screening
- **Instant screening** - Real-time background checks
- **Batch processing** - Multiple applicant screening
- **Custom criteria** - Property-specific screening requirements
- **Risk assessment** - Automated risk scoring
