# Screening and Fraud Prevention

The Screening and Fraud Prevention module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides comprehensive background checks, financial analysis, and identity verification to ensure secure and compliant leasing decisions.

## Overview

Our advanced screening system combines multiple verification methods to provide thorough applicant assessment while preventing fraud and ensuring compliance with fair housing laws.

## Key Features

### Comprehensive Screening
- **Multi-factor verification** - Multiple screening methods for complete assessment
- **Automated processing** - Streamlined screening workflows
- **Risk assessment** - Automated risk scoring and evaluation
- **Compliance monitoring** - Fair housing and legal compliance

### Fraud Prevention
- **Advanced fraud detection** - Sophisticated algorithms to identify fraud
- **Identity verification** - Multi-layer identity confirmation
- **Document validation** - Authentic document verification
- **Behavioral analysis** - Suspicious activity monitoring
