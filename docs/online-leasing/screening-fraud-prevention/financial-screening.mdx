# Financial Screening

The Financial Screening module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides detailed financial analysis, income verification, and financial risk assessment to ensure applicants meet financial requirements.

## Overview

Our comprehensive financial screening system evaluates applicants' financial capacity, income stability, and payment history to help property managers make informed leasing decisions.

## Key Features

### Income Verification
- **Employment verification** - Current and previous employment
- **Income documentation** - Pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements
- **Multiple income sources** - Secondary employment, investments
- **Income stability** - Employment history and consistency

### Financial Analysis
- **Credit score assessment** - Financial responsibility evaluation
- **Debt-to-income ratio** - Financial capacity calculation
- **Payment history** - Bill payment patterns and consistency
- **Financial stability** - Long-term financial health

### Risk Assessment
- **Financial risk scoring** - Automated risk evaluation
- **Income adequacy** - Rent-to-income ratio analysis
- **Payment capacity** - Ability to meet financial obligations
- **Financial red flags** - Potential financial issues identification

## Screening Components

### Income Verification
- **Current employment** - Active employment verification
- **Income amount** - Gross and net income verification
- **Employment stability** - Job tenure and consistency
- **Future income** - Expected income changes

### Credit Analysis
- **Credit score** - FICO or VantageScore assessment
- **Payment history** - Credit account payment patterns
- **Credit utilization** - Available credit usage
- **Credit inquiries** - Recent credit applications

### Financial Capacity
- **Debt obligations** - Current debt payments
- **Living expenses** - Monthly expense analysis
- **Savings and assets** - Financial reserves
- **Financial goals** - Long-term financial planning

## Financial Metrics

### Income-to-Rent Ratio
- **Standard ratio** - 3:1 gross income to rent
- **Flexible ratios** - Property-specific requirements
- **Multiple income sources** - Combined income calculation
- **Future income** - Expected income changes

### Debt-to-Income Ratio
- **Total debt ratio** - All debt payments to income
- **Housing ratio** - Housing costs to income
- **Acceptable limits** - Property-specific thresholds
- **Risk assessment** - Financial capacity evaluation

### Credit Score Analysis
- **Excellent credit** - 750+ credit score
- **Good credit** - 700-749 credit score
- **Fair credit** - 650-699 credit score
- **Poor credit** - Below 650 credit score
