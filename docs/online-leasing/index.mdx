# Online Leasing Overview

Welcome to the Funnel Leasing Online Leasing documentation. Our digital leasing platform streamlines the entire application process, from initial inquiry to lease signing, making it easier for both property managers and applicants.

## What is Funnel Leasing Online Leasing?

Funnel Leasing Online Leasing is a comprehensive digital platform that transforms the traditional leasing process into a seamless, online experience. It enables:

- **Lease transactions** - Complete lifecycle management from application to move-out
- **Screening and fraud prevention** - Comprehensive background checks and identity verification
- **Legal and contract management** - Automated document generation and digital signing
- **Integration** - Seamless connection with CRM and other systems

## Key Features

### Lease Transactions
- Complete lifecycle management from application to move-out
- Automated workflows for renewals, transfers, and revisions
- Financial reconciliation and accounting
- Status tracking and communication tools

### Screening and Fraud Prevention
- Comprehensive background and financial screening
- Multi-factor identity verification with fraud detection
- Risk assessment and automated decision support
- Legal compliance with fair housing laws

### Legal and Contracts
- Automated document generation with template library
- Digital signature processing with legal compliance
- Legal notice generation and delivery
- Secure document storage and audit trails

## Getting Started

1. **Configure your properties** - Set up property listings and requirements
2. **Set up transaction workflows** - Configure lease transaction processes
3. **Configure screening criteria** - Set up background and financial requirements
4. **Set up legal templates** - Configure document templates and compliance
5. **Test the process** - Ensure everything works smoothly
