# Notices

The Notices module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing provides automated generation and delivery of legal notices required throughout the leasing process.

## Overview

Our digital notice system ensures proper legal compliance while streamlining the notice generation and delivery process for all lease-related communications.

## Key Features

### Automated Notice Generation
- **Template-based creation** - Pre-approved notice templates
- **Legal compliance** - Built-in legal requirement checking
- **Customization options** - Property-specific notice content
- **Delivery tracking** - Complete notice delivery history

### Notice Types
- **Lease notices** - Termination and renewal notices
- **Compliance notices** - Required legal disclosures
- **Operational notices** - Maintenance and policy updates
- **Emergency notices** - Urgent communications

### Delivery Methods
- **Digital delivery** - Email, SMS, and in-app notifications
- **Physical delivery** - Certified mail and personal delivery
- **Posting notices** - Door posting and bulletin boards
- **Electronic posting** - Online portals and digital boards
