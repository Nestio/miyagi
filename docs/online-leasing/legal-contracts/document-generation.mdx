# Document Generation

The Document Generation module in Funnel Leasing Online Leasing automates the creation, customization, and management of lease agreements and related legal documents.

## Overview

Our digital document generation system streamlines the entire document lifecycle, from template creation to final execution, ensuring legal compliance and operational efficiency.

## Key Features

### Document Generation
- **Template library** - Pre-approved document templates
- **Dynamic content** - Automatic data population
- **Customization tools** - Property-specific modifications
- **Legal compliance** - State and local law compliance

### Document Types
- **Lease agreements** - Standard and custom lease documents
- **Addendums and amendments** - Lease modifications and addendums
- **Supporting documents** - Move-in checklists and welcome packets
- **Legal notices** - Required legal notifications

### Document Management
- **Version control** - Document version tracking
- **Secure storage** - Encrypted document storage
- **Access controls** - Role-based document access
- **Document routing** - Automated document workflows
