# CRM Overview

Welcome to the Funnel Leasing CRM documentation. Our CRM system is designed specifically for property management professionals to streamline lead management, prospect tracking, and deal pipeline management.

## What is Funnel Leasing CRM?

Funnel Leasing CRM is a comprehensive customer relationship management solution built specifically for the property management industry. It helps property managers and leasing agents:

- **Track leads** from initial contact to lease signing
- **Manage prospects** through the entire leasing funnel
- **Monitor deal pipelines** with real-time analytics
- **Generate reports** and insights for better decision making
- **Integrate seamlessly** with other Funnel Leasing products

## Key Features

### Lead Management
- Capture leads from multiple sources (website, phone, walk-ins)
- Automated lead scoring and qualification
- Lead assignment and follow-up scheduling
- Lead nurturing campaigns

### Prospect Tracking
- Detailed prospect profiles and history
- Communication tracking and logging
- Property preferences and requirements
- Tour scheduling and management

### Deal Pipeline
- Visual pipeline management
- Stage-based deal tracking
- Conversion rate analytics
- Deal forecasting and projections

### Reporting & Analytics
- Real-time dashboards
- Custom report builder
- Performance metrics
- ROI tracking
