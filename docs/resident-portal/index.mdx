# Resident Portal Overview

Welcome to the Funnel Leasing Resident Portal documentation. Our resident portal provides a comprehensive self-service platform that empowers residents to manage their accounts, submit maintenance requests, and access community features.

## What is Funnel Leasing Resident Portal?

The Funnel Leasing Resident Portal is a modern, user-friendly platform designed to enhance the resident experience by providing convenient access to essential services and information. It enables residents to:

- **Manage accounts** - View and update personal information
- **Submit maintenance requests** - Report issues and track progress
- **Access payment history** - View past payments and statements
- **Connect with community** - Access community features and announcements
- **Mobile access** - Use the portal on any device

## Key Features

### Account Management
- Personal profile management
- Contact information updates
- Emergency contact management
- Document storage and access

### Maintenance Requests
- Easy issue reporting
- Photo and video uploads
- Request tracking and updates
- Communication with maintenance team
- Priority-based scheduling

### Payment History
- Complete payment history
- Download statements and receipts
- Payment method management
- Late fee tracking and explanations

### Community Features
- Community announcements
- Event calendar
- Resident directory (with privacy controls)
- Community guidelines and policies
- Feedback and suggestions

## Getting Started

1. **Set up your account** - Create your resident profile
2. **Complete your profile** - Add emergency contacts and preferences
3. **Explore features** - Familiarize yourself with available services
4. **Download mobile app** - Access the portal on the go
