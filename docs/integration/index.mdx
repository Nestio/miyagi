Funnel Leasing provides a suite of embedded widgets designed to optimize lead tracking and marketing attribution for your website. These widgets seamlessly integrate into your site to capture leads, schedule appointments, provide AI-powered chat support, and dynamically update phone numbers based on marketing sources.

## What are Website Widgets?

Website widgets are JavaScript-based components that you embed directly into your website's HTML. They enable you to:

- **Capture leads** - Collect visitor information through customizable lead forms
- **Schedule appointments** - Allow prospects to book appointments with your leasing team
- **Provide AI chat support** - Offer instant assistance through an AI-powered chatbot
- **Track marketing attribution** - Dynamically update phone numbers and capture campaign data based on UTM parameters and referrers

## Available Widgets

| Widget | Description | Use Case |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Lead Form** | Captures visitor contact information and creates leads in Funnel Leasing | Collect basic lead information from website visitors |
| **Appointment Scheduler** | Allows prospects to schedule appointments with leasing professionals | Enable self-service appointment booking |
| **Chatbot** | AI-powered chat widget that answers questions and captures leads | Provide 24/7 automated support and lead capture |
| **Dynamic Number Insertion (DNI)** | Updates phone numbers on your site based on marketing sources | Track marketing attribution and display campaign-specific phone numbers |

## Getting Started

To get started with website widgets:

1. **Obtain your credentials** - Contact your Funnel Leasing account representative or support team to get your API key and Community/Group IDs
2. **Choose your widgets** - Determine which widgets best fit your website's needs
3. **Embed the code** - Add the widget script tags to your website's HTML
4. **Configure options** - Customize widget appearance and behavior using configuration parameters
5. **Test and validate** - Verify that widgets are working correctly on your site

## Prerequisites

Before integrating widgets, ensure you have:

- **Funnel Leasing account** - An active Funnel Leasing account with appropriate permissions
- **API Key** - Your Funnel Leasing Customer API key (provided by your account representative)
- **Community/Group IDs** - The IDs for the communities and groups you want to associate with widgets
- **Website access** - Ability to modify your website's HTML to add script tags

## Widget Integration Overview

Each widget follows a similar integration pattern:

1. **Add script tags** - Include the widget's JavaScript file in your HTML
2. **Initialize the widget** - Call the widget's initialization function with your configuration
3. **Customize appearance** - Set colors, labels, and other styling options
4. **Handle callbacks** - Use callback functions to trigger analytics events or custom logic
