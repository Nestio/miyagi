# AI Virtual Assistant Overview

Welcome to the Funnel Leasing AI Virtual Assistant documentation. Our intelligent chatbot solution helps property managers automate customer interactions, qualify leads, and provide 24/7 support to prospects and residents.

## What is Funnel Leasing AI Virtual Assistant?

The Funnel Leasing AI Virtual Assistant is an advanced conversational AI platform designed specifically for the property management industry. It leverages natural language processing and machine learning to:

- **Automate customer interactions** - Handle common inquiries 24/7
- **Qualify leads** - Identify and score potential prospects
- **Schedule appointments** - Book tours and meetings automatically
- **Provide instant support** - Answer questions about properties and policies
- **Integrate seamlessly** - Connect with CRM and other systems
