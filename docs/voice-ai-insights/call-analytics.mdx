# Call Analytics

The Funnel Leasing Voice AI & Insights platform provides comprehensive call analytics that transform phone conversations into actionable business intelligence. Our advanced analytics help property managers optimize communication strategies and improve customer satisfaction.

## Call Recording and Transcription

### Automatic Recording
Seamless call capture and storage:

- **Automatic recording** - Record all incoming and outgoing calls
- **Quality optimization** - High-quality audio capture and storage
- **Secure storage** - Encrypted storage with compliance features
- **Retention policies** - Configurable retention periods
- **Access controls** - Role-based access to recordings

### Speech-to-Text Conversion
Advanced transcription capabilities:

- **Real-time transcription** - Live transcription during calls
- **Multi-language support** - Transcribe calls in multiple languages
- **Speaker identification** - Distinguish between different speakers
- **Punctuation and formatting** - Intelligent text formatting
- **Custom vocabulary** - Industry-specific terminology recognition

### Quality Assurance
Ensure transcription accuracy:

- **Confidence scoring** - Measure transcription accuracy
- **Manual review** - Human review of low-confidence transcriptions
- **Correction tools** - Easy editing and correction interface
- **Quality metrics** - Track transcription quality over time
- **Continuous improvement** - Learn from corrections and feedback

## Performance Analytics

### Call Volume Metrics
Track call activity and patterns:

- **Total calls** - Number of calls received and made
- **Call duration** - Average and total call times
- **Peak hours** - Identify busiest call periods
- **Seasonal trends** - Analyze call patterns over time
- **Geographic distribution** - Call volume by location

### Agent Performance
Monitor individual and team performance:

- **Call handling time** - Average time to resolve issues
- **First call resolution** - Percentage of issues resolved on first call
- **Customer satisfaction** - Post-call satisfaction scores
- **Call quality scores** - Automated quality assessment
- **Productivity metrics** - Calls per hour, efficiency ratings

### Customer Experience
Measure customer satisfaction and engagement:

- **Satisfaction scores** - Post-call customer ratings
- **Sentiment analysis** - Emotional tone of conversations
- **Issue resolution** - Success rate in resolving problems
- **Wait times** - Time customers spend on hold
- **Call outcomes** - Successful vs. unsuccessful call results
