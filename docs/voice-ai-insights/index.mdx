# Voice AI & Insights Overview

Welcome to the Funnel Leasing Voice AI & Insights documentation. Our advanced voice analytics platform provides deep insights into customer interactions, helping property managers optimize their communication strategies and improve customer satisfaction.

## What is Funnel Leasing Voice AI & Insights?

Funnel Leasing Voice AI & Insights is a comprehensive voice analytics platform that transforms phone conversations into actionable business intelligence. It uses advanced AI to:

- **Analyze call quality** - Monitor and improve communication effectiveness
- **Extract insights** - Identify trends and patterns in customer interactions
- **Measure sentiment** - Understand customer emotions and satisfaction
- **Track performance** - Monitor agent performance and training needs
- **Generate reports** - Create detailed analytics and recommendations

## Key Features

### Call Analytics
- Call recording and transcription
- Quality scoring and evaluation
- Performance metrics tracking
- Compliance monitoring
- Real-time analytics dashboard

### Sentiment Analysis
- Emotion detection and analysis
- Customer satisfaction scoring
- Sentiment trend tracking
- Alert system for negative interactions
- Improvement recommendations

### Performance Metrics
- Agent performance evaluation
- Call handling efficiency
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Training needs identification
- Performance benchmarking

### Voice Recognition
- Speech-to-text conversion
- Keyword and phrase detection
- Intent recognition
- Multi-language support
- Custom vocabulary training
